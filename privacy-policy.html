Privacy Policy

Preamble

This Privacy Policy describes how the SocialVault mobile application ("the Application"), developed by Benashe Softworks ("the Studio"), collects, uses and protects user information. By using SocialVault, you agree to the practices described in this document.

SocialVault is an Android application that allows downloading media from social platforms (Facebook, X/Twitter, Instagram, Pinterest, TikTok), saving WhatsApp statuses, converting video files into audio files, and securely storing personal files in a local, PIN or password-protected Vault.

Article 1 — Data Controller Identity

Studio: Benashe Softworks
Contact email: benashesoftworks@gmail.com
Capacity: Independent studio, sole data controller for the SocialVault Application.

Article 2 — Data Collected

2.1 No collection of personal data

SocialVault does not collect any personally identifiable data. No account, name, email or phone number is required.

2.2 Anonymized technical data

Anonymized technical data may be collected by integrated third-party services (e.g. Firebase Crashlytics) for performance purposes.

2.3 Premium subscription data

Payments are entirely managed by Google Play Billing. Benashe Softworks does not collect, process or store any banking information.

SocialVault never sees your payment information.

Article 3 — Access to Device Media Folders

3.1 Saving WhatsApp statuses

SocialVault reads the local WhatsApp storage directory, strictly limited to temporary status files.

No data is transmitted to external servers or stored outside the device.

3.2 Saving downloaded media

Videos (Facebook, X, Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest) and images (TikTok, Instagram) downloaded through the Application are saved locally and never transmitted to servers.

3.3 Video-to-Audio conversion

The converter extracts the audio track (MP3) from a video file (MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, WebM) already present on the device. Conversion is performed entirely offline, with no upload to any external server.

Article 4 — Vault: Local Secure Storage

The Vault feature allows moving photos, videos, audio and documents into a protected, hidden space within the Application.

4.1 Local-only storage

All Vault files remain stored exclusively in the device's local memory. No Vault content is transmitted to Benashe Softworks or to any third-party server.

4.2 Access protection

Access is protected by a 4-digit PIN code or a password, processed locally via a cryptographic hash, secured via Android Keystore where supported.

4.3 No storage or recovery of the PIN/password by the Studio

  • the Studio cannot recover, reset or bypass a forgotten PIN or password;
  • loss of the PIN/password may result in permanent loss of access to Vault files;
  • the user is solely responsible for remembering their credential and keeping independent backups of important files.

The Vault is a local protection tool, not a backup or cloud storage service.

Article 5 — Security: Protected Content Access Policy

5.1 Systematic blocking of protected content

The Application automatically detects links pointing to protected content (private Stories, locked profiles, private Pinterest boards, etc.) and blocks their processing.

5.2 Complete absence of login interface

SocialVault has no credential entry interface for third-party platforms.

SocialVault will NEVER ask for your credentials on Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest or X.

Article 6 — No External Data Transmission

  • no personal data stored on remote servers;
  • no data sold, rented or transferred to third parties;
  • downloaded or saved media remains on the user's device at all times;
  • Vault files and converter files never leave the user's device;
  • no outgoing network access to personal data, except downloading public media from its original URL.

Article 7 — Advertising (Free Version)

The free version includes ads provided by third-party networks (e.g. Google AdMob), which may use the Android Advertising ID (AAID). The Premium subscription removes all advertising.

Article 8 — Required Android Permissions

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO: WhatsApp statuses and Vault media;
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: saving media to the device gallery;
  • INTERNET: downloading media from social platforms;
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: download and conversion progress notifications;
  • BILLING: managing the Premium subscription.

Article 9 — User Rights

The user has rights of access, rectification, erasure and objection regarding data potentially collected by integrated third-party services.

Article 10 — Policy Changes

Benashe Softworks may modify this Policy at any time. In the event of a material change, the user will be informed via the Application or the Google Play Store.
